# Self Apply Center Brand Assets

Send or place the final brand files here so the website can be updated accurately.

## Needed

- Logo file: SVG preferred, PNG/WebP also fine
- Primary brand color: example `#0C2143`
- Secondary brand color: example `#2563EB`
- Accent color: example `#10B8A6`
- Social links: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, or X
- Business contact: email, phone/WhatsApp, office address

## Where Logo Will Go

Place the logo inside:

```text
assets/
```

Example:

```text
assets/sac-logo.png
```

The current cropped logo is already saved as:

```text
assets/sac-logo.png
```

## Where Colors Will Change

Brand colors are controlled near the top of:

```text
styles.css
```

Look for the `:root` section.
