Quick answers

Before you message us, these answers may help.

These FAQs cover common first questions from students planning for the USA, Canada, UK, South Korea, and other study destinations.

Can I start if I am not sure about my country?

Yes. SAC can begin with profile diagnosis and help compare destinations based on academic fit, budget, course direction, and timeline.

Do you help with USA and Canada applications?

Yes. USA and Canada are core SAC pathways, alongside the UK and South Korea. We help with course shortlisting, document review, applications, and next-step planning.

Do you guide students for the UK and South Korea?

Yes. SAC can help compare UK and South Korea options based on course length, entry requirements, budget, intake timing, and future career direction.

What documents should I prepare first?

Start with your passport, academic transcripts, certificates, English test score if available, CV, statement of purpose draft, recommendation letters, and financial documents.

Can SAC review my SOP, CV, and documents?

Yes. We can review your documents for clarity, consistency, missing details, and alignment with your chosen course and destination.

Can I apply if I have a study gap?

Possibly. A study gap does not automatically stop an application, but it needs a clear explanation, supporting documents, and a destination/course plan that makes sense.

Do you help after I receive an offer letter?

Yes. SAC can guide students with offer conditions, enrollment steps, financial preparation, visa documents, interview preparation when needed, and pre-departure planning.

How do I start my application?

You can start through the Apply Now button, send your details through the contact form, or message SAC directly on WhatsApp with your preferred country, course, and education background.

Where is the SAC office?

Self Apply Center is located at Star Mall, 2nd Floor, Putalisadak, Kathmandu.

What is the fastest way to contact SAC?

Use WhatsApp at +977 9761642336 or call the office at 01-4012581.
