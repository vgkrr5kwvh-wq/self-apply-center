Student FAQ

Common questions before starting an application.

These quick answers help students understand what SAC needs from them before counselling or application review.

Which country should I choose first?

Start with your course goal, budget, academic history, and career plan. Then compare USA, Canada, UK, and South Korea against those facts.

Do I need IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, or Duolingo?

It depends on the country, university, program level, and your academic background. SAC can help identify which test route makes sense for your shortlist.

When should I begin preparing?

Ideally, start 6 to 12 months before your target intake so you have enough time for documents, testing, course selection, and visa preparation.

Can SAC help me choose a course?

Yes. SAC can help connect your previous study, interests, budget, and future role to practical course options.
